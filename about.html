About i-bookworm

Some information about us.

i-bookworm is about fostering diverse community engagement. You can connect with readers of all ages and backgrounds, creating an inclusive community for sharing book recommendations.

In our virtual book clubs, you can join interactive discussions and feel a sense of community among readers. Clubs cater to readers with diverse interests in various genres and subjects and who have an appreciation for ‘social reading’.

Book clubs not for you? i-bookworm is a literary adventure! You can fly solo. You can expand your horizons, discover new authors and foster your love for literature and knowledge - in the i-bookworm community, but on your own!
